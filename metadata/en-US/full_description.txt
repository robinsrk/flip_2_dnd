Flip 2 DND is a simple yet powerful app that automatically toggles Do Not Disturb mode based on your phone's orientation.

Features:

- Toggle DND by flipping your phone face down
- Disable DND by flipping your phone face up
- Optional "Only when screen off" mode
- Configurable vibration feedback
- Sound notification when DND is disabled
- Clean, modern Material You design
- No ads, no tracking, completely open source

The app is designed with privacy and simplicity in mind, requiring only the necessary permissions to function:

- Access to Do Not Disturb settings
- Vibration control
- Sensor access (accelerometer)

Perfect for meetings, study sessions, or any time you need quick access to Do Not Disturb mode!
